# DeciRepo FAQ v0.1

---

### Q: Is DeciRepo a blockchain?
**A:** No. It does not use global consensus or a distributed ledger to order transactions. It uses **deterministic recomputation** to verify individual artifacts. It is "Git for decisions," not "Bitcoin for decisions."

### Q: Is this Explainable AI (XAI)?
**A:** No. AI is inherently non-deterministic. DeciRepo works with **fixed, versioned policy models**. It provides a "reasoning trace" based on logic, not statistical probability.

### Q: Why do we need another registry?
**A:** Current registries store *data*. DeciRepo stores *proofs*. A traditional audit log tells you *that* something happened. A DeciRepo artifact allows you to prove *why* the system allowed it to happen.

### Q: Who operates the nodes?
**A:** The network is federated. Any trusted authority—audit firms, certification bodies, or infrastructure providers—can operate a node to provide independent verification capacity.

### Q: What is the main benefit for a business?
**A:** Radical reduction in audit costs and near-instant resolution of decision disputes. You move from a state of "constant preparation for audit" to a state of "continuous verifiability."
