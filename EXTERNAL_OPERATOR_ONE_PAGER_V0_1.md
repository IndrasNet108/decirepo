# External Operator One-Pager v0.1

### What is DeciRepo?
DeciRepo is a federated decision verification infrastructure based on **proof by recomputation**. It transforms institutional decisions into immutable, reproducible digital artifacts.

### Why does this matter?
Currently, institutional decisions (compliance, risk, approvals) are trapped in logs, emails, and PDFs. They are hard to verify and impossible to reproduce. DeciRepo makes them as verifiable as a git commit.

### Why should we join?
As an operator, you provide the independent "second look" that builds systemic trust. You transition from a consultant who reads reports to a verification node that runs proofs.

### What do we need to run?
- **Hardware:** 4 cores, 8GB RAM, 100GB Storage.
- **Software:** DeciRepo node implementation + `dlx-ref` engine.
- **Connectivity:** Public HTTPS endpoint.

### What do we get?
- Participation in the **Global Verification Graph**.
- Visibility as a trusted network verifier.
- Ability to monetize high-assurance verification services.
- Deterministic, machine-readable evidence for audit and compliance.

**DeciRepo: The Verification Layer for Institutional Decisions.**
