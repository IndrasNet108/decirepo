# First Real Operator List v0.1

**Status:** Active Target List  
**Objective:** Genesis Network Onboarding (Months 1–12)

---

## 1. Purpose
Define the first named operator targets for the DeciRepo proto-federation launch. These organizations have been selected based on their technical fit (API-first, evidence automation) and their market role as verification authorities.

---

## 2. Scoring Rubric (1–5)
- **Technical Fit:** Readiness to integrate with deterministic artifacts and APIs.
- **Credibility:** Existing reputation as a verifier or compliance authority.
- **Speed:** Agility to launch a technical pilot.
- **Network Value:** Impact of their participation on global trust.

---

## 6. Non-Targets for First Wave
- **The Big Four (Deloitte, PwC, etc.):** Too slow for initial pilot stage.
- **Governments:** Regulatory overhead exceeds early-stage capacity.
- **Hyperscalers (AWS/Azure):** Their internal policy logic is too opaque for early federation.

## 7. 90-Day Outreach Order
1. **Days 1–30:** Focus exclusively on **Sprinto, Vanta, and A-LIGN**. Goal: 1 pilot commitment.
2. **Days 31–60:** Expand to **Drata and Secureframe**. Goal: 2 technical workshops.
3. **Days 61–90:** Begin informational touchpoints with **Tier 2 (Schellman/Coalfire)**.
