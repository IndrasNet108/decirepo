About DeciRepo

Bounded workflow validation powered by DLX.

What this is

DeciRepo runs a bounded pilot on one defined workflow surface. The pilot is scoped before it starts, follows a reproducible validation path, and ends with a documented output that can be reviewed inside an internal decision process.

What the pilot does

Tests one workflow boundary on one defined surface
Checks whether reviewed wording and outbound wording can diverge at commit
Produces a documented package for internal review
Supports internal decision-making without replacing it

What the pilot does not do

It does not provide legal advice
It does not approve policy or transfer authority
It does not run broad rollout or implementation work
It does not perform open-ended discovery across the organization

When this fits

Near-miss
A workflow almost created a position no one meant to approve.
Recurring issue
The same ambiguity appears again and again in one workflow surface.
Audit or review pressure
A team needs a documented boundary and a reviewable result.
Rollout checkpoint
A workflow is about to scale before its approval boundary is explicit.

What you receive

Boundary Decision Pack: the scoped boundary call, key findings, and review path.
Executive Pilot Summary: a short readout that can be forwarded internally.
Workflow Boundary Report: the detailed record of the workflow surface that was tested.
Evidence Appendix: the dated artifacts and trace used in the review.
Next-Step Decision Note: the bounded options for what happens next.

The output is structured to support internal review and next-step discussion without turning the system into decision authority.
